Recipes

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients: Spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, chili flakes

Instructions: Cook spaghetti, sauté garlic in oil, add chili, mix and serve.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, tomato paste, cream, butter, spices

Instructions: Marinate chicken, cook in sauce, add cream and serve with naan.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice – 2 cups, Chicken – 500g, Onions, tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, etc.), Saffron milk – optional

Instructions:Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices. Fry onions, cook chicken halfway. Layer rice and chicken, add saffron milk. Cover and steam (dum) for 20 mins.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients:Romaine lettuce, Croutons, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing.

Instructions: Wash and cut lettuce. Add croutons and cheese. Toss with Caesar dressing and serve chilled.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:Paneer cubes – 300g, Yogurt – ½ cup, Tikka masala – 2 tbsp, Bell peppers, onions – cubed.

Instructions: Mix yogurt with spices. Marinate paneer and veggies. Skewer and grill/bake until charred. Serve with mint chutney.

Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:Flour – 1 cup, Eggs – 2, Milk – ¾ cup, Baking powder – 2 tsp, Sugar, salt.

Instructions: Mix dry and wet ingredients separately. Combine smoothly. Pour on hot pan and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook. Serve with syrup and fruits.

Ramen Noodles

Ingredients:Ramen noodles, Broth – chicken/veg, Soy sauce, miso paste, garlic, Egg, spring onion, mushrooms.

Instructions: Prepare broth with flavorings. Boil noodles separately. Assemble with toppings: egg, scallions, etc.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:Pizza dough, Tomato sauce, Fresh mozzarella, Basil leaves, Olive oil.

Instructions:Season steak and sear both sides. Finish in oven if needed. Serve with veggies and sauce.
